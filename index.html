About Us

Your Trusted Partner in Refrigerated Transport

MNW For Transport by Refrigerator Trucks is a licensed transport company specializing in temperature-controlled logistics solutions. Based in Dubai, we provide reliable refrigerated transport services for various industries including food, pharmaceuticals, and other temperature-sensitive goods.

With our modern fleet of refrigerated trucks and experienced team, we ensure your products are transported under optimal temperature conditions, maintaining their quality and integrity throughout the journey.

Our commitment to customer satisfaction and follow-up service sets us apart in the industry. We prioritize communication and ensure that every delivery meets our clients' expectations.

Licensed by the Department of Economic Development in Dubai (License No. 1494032), we adhere to the highest standards of quality and safety in all our operations.

Our Services

Refrigerated Transport

Our core service provides temperature-controlled transport for perishable and temperature-sensitive goods. We maintain precise temperature settings throughout transit to ensure product integrity.

Ideal for: Food products, pharmaceuticals, flowers, and other temperature-sensitive items.

Heavy Cargo Transport

We offer heavy-duty transport solutions for larger shipments that require specialized handling and equipment. Our heavy trucks are equipped to handle substantial loads safely and efficiently.

Suitable for: Bulk food shipments, large medical equipment, and other substantial cargo requiring temperature control.

Light Cargo Transport

For smaller shipments and urban deliveries, our light refrigerated trucks provide flexible and efficient transport solutions, navigating through city traffic with ease.

Perfect for: Small business deliveries, catering services, pharmaceutical deliveries, and time-sensitive urban logistics.

Our Commitment to Customer Satisfaction

What sets us apart is our dedication to customer follow-up and satisfaction. We don't just deliver your goods; we ensure the entire process meets your expectations. Our team maintains communication throughout the transport process and follows up after delivery to ensure complete satisfaction.

Our Fleet

Heavy Refrigerated Trucks

Our heavy-duty refrigerated trucks are designed for large-volume shipments requiring precise temperature control.

  • Temperature Range: -25°C to +25°C
  • Capacity: Up to 10 tons
  • Features: Multi-temperature zones, GPS tracking

Medium Refrigerated Trucks

Versatile medium-sized refrigerated vehicles for balanced capacity and maneuverability.

  • Temperature Range: -20°C to +20°C
  • Capacity: 3-5 tons
  • Features: Digital temperature monitoring, backup power

Light Refrigerated Trucks

Compact refrigerated vehicles ideal for urban deliveries and smaller shipments.

  • Temperature Range: -15°C to +15°C
  • Capacity: Up to 2 tons
  • Features: Agile design, fuel efficiency, quick cooling

Fleet Features & Maintenance

All our vehicles undergo regular maintenance and cleaning to ensure reliability and hygiene. Each truck is equipped with temperature monitoring systems, GPS tracking, and backup power to guarantee the safety of your goods during transit.
